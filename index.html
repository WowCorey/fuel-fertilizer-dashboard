Status legend

Status labels are part of the evidence

Unavailable and source-gated data is not a dashboard failure. It is evidence of a public-data gap. Hover any pill on this site to see its meaning.

Verified
Source-backed and current enough for its cadence.
Partial
Source-backed, but incomplete by geography, product, timing or concept.
Stale
Source-backed, but outside expected cadence.
Manual
Hand-keyed from a named public source.
Derived
Calculated or selected from a named source envelope.
Unavailable
No public source-safe feed is loaded.
Source-gated
Waiting for verified source, field, period, unit and reuse rights.
Roadmap
Planned dashboard area, not yet populated.

National dashboard summary

A 30-second view of national resilience signals

This site is an independent public-source prototype, not an official government dashboard. It shows what can be verified from public sources and keeps missing feeds visible instead of filling gaps with estimates.

Partial

Fuel security

Days-cover, MSO context and aggregate import visibility wired; live operational data still missing.

Missing feed: live station-level availability, terminal inventory, tanker ETAs and forward contracts.

Why it matters: fuel affects freight, farms, emergency services, defence posture and regional resilience.

Last updated: source metadata pending

Source-gated

Food, farms and water

Fertiliser imports and source concentration loaded; farm diesel, water and drought feeds source-gated.

Missing feed: fertiliser stock cover, farm diesel risk, water allocation by food-producing region.

Why it matters: fuel, fertiliser and water determine whether farms can produce and move food.

Last updated: source metadata pending

Partial

Economy and housing pressure

Latest RBA cash-rate target and selected macro indicators are verified; housing models stay source-gated.

Missing feed: mortgage repayment, rental stress, investor ownership, first-home buyer and negative-gearing models.

Why it matters: rates, debt and housing pressure shape cost of living and social stability.

Last updated: source metadata pending

Partial

Power, manufacturing and infrastructure

AEMO regional price/demand and ABS infrastructure indicators wired; resilience and project-delivery views still missing.

Missing feed: outage resilience, manufacturing bottlenecks, project delivery status, logistics constraints.

Why it matters: resilience depends on power reliability, industrial capacity and infrastructure delivery.

Last updated: source metadata pending

Source-gated

Defence and strategic resources

Public capability rows, alliance frameworks and critical-mineral production tables loaded; operational defence detail unavailable.

Missing feed: verified procurement contracts, delivery rows, naval logistics implications, critical mineral project readiness.

Why it matters: fuel, ships, resources and industrial capacity are national-security issues.

Last updated: source metadata pending

Data policy

No fabricated numbers

If a value cannot be verified from a named public source, the relevant dashboard renders a placeholder instead of an estimate. Manual values should only be entered after checking the publisher document listed in the source registry.

Source rights

Publisher terms still apply

Project code, prose and metadata have their own licences. Upstream source data remains under each publisher's rights and citation requirements.

Next data priorities

Roadmap gaps stay visible until source-safe

The next data work is fuel strategy and policy releases, Queensland fuel-sovereignty follow-up, live station and terminal operations, food/farm/water planning feeds, housing pressure, AI workforce exposure and Brisbane 2032 readiness.

Source gates

No estimate-first roadmap

Warship procurement watch items, forward contracts, state-owned land, regional fuel risk, farm diesel and water-allocation feeds remain source-gated until official/public sources expose exact fields, dates, units and reuse rights.
